## LinkedIn "About" (long)
Graider is an AI-powered grading and planning assistant built for grades 6–12 teachers. It grades student assignments — typed in our student portal, or uploaded as Word, PDF, or photos of handwritten work — in minutes, with detailed per-question feedback the teacher reviews and approves before anything is finalized. Beyond grading, Graider generates standards-aligned lesson plans, builds worksheets and assessments, and surfaces real-time class analytics, combining the full teaching loop in one tool. It runs on GPT-4o, Claude, or Gemini (teacher's choice), integrates with Clever, ClassLink, and OneRoster for rostering and SSO, supports IEP/504 accommodations and ELL feedback, and is FERPA-compliant. Built in the United States for US K-12 classrooms.
