Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Graider is designed with privacy as a core principle. We understand that educators handle sensitive student information, and we've built our application to ensure that data stays where it belongs: on your computer.

FERPA Compliance Summary

Graider is designed to be FERPA compliant. Student names are never sent to AI services. All student work, grades, and feedback remain on your local device. We do not store, access, or process any student educational records.

How Graider Works

Graider is a desktop application that runs entirely on your computer. Here's what this means for your data:

  • Local Processing: All file reading, parsing, and storage happens on your device
  • Your API Key: You provide your own OpenAI API key, which means API calls go directly from your computer to OpenAI
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not operate servers that store your data
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account to use Graider

Data We Collect

Beta Signup Information

When you sign up for our beta program through our website, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address

This information is collected via Formspree and is used solely to provide you with download links, updates about Graider, and occasional product announcements. You can unsubscribe at any time.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Student names or identifiers
  • Student work or assignments
  • Grades or feedback
  • Rubrics or assignment configurations
  • Any content you process through Graider
  • Usage analytics or telemetry from the desktop app

AI Processing & Student Privacy

When Graider sends content to OpenAI for grading:

  • Student names are stripped: We automatically remove student names before sending any content to the AI
  • Direct API connection: Content goes directly from your computer to OpenAI using your API key
  • No intermediary servers: Graider does not route your data through our servers
  • OpenAI's policies apply: Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for how they handle API requests

Data Storage

All Graider data is stored locally on your computer in the following locations:

  • ~/.graider_rubric.json - Your rubric settings
  • ~/.graider_settings.json - Application settings
  • ~/.graider_assignments/ - Saved assignment configurations
  • ~/.graider_email.json - Email credentials (if configured)

You have full control over this data. You can delete these files at any time to remove all Graider data from your computer.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

Graider uses OpenAI's API for AI-powered grading. When you use Graider:

  • Assignment content (without student names) is sent to OpenAI
  • Your API key is used for authentication
  • OpenAI's data handling policies apply to this content

Formspree

Our website uses Formspree to handle beta signup forms. Formspree processes your name and email according to their privacy policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we have about you (limited to beta signup data)
  • Request deletion of your information from our beta signup list
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time
  • Delete all local Graider data by removing the configuration files listed above

Children's Privacy

Graider is designed for use by educators, not students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The application processes student work, but this data never leaves the educator's computer or reaches our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
