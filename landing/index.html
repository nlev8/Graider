Features

Everything you need to grade smarter

Powerful AI tools designed specifically for educators, built to understand academic context and maintain your teaching standards.

Lightning Fast Grading

Grade 100 assignments in minutes. Word docs, PDFs, even handwritten work via photo. AI maintains consistent quality at any scale.

Academic Integrity Detection

Multi-layer AI detection: phrase analysis, writing profiles, contrast detection. Catches AI-generated work and plagiarism automatically.

Bilingual Feedback (ELL)

Auto-detect Spanish, Creole, Portuguese responses. Deliver feedback in English + native language so every student understands.

IEP/504 Accommodations

Built-in presets: simplified language, effort-focused, chunked feedback. Same assignment, personalized feedback for every learner.

Student Progress Tracking

Build writing profiles over time. Track performance trends, skill patterns, and detect sudden changes that signal AI use.

AI Lesson Planner

Generate complete lesson plans from standards. Includes bell ringers, activities, assessments, and vocabulary - all in seconds.

FERPA Compliant

Student names never sent to AI. All data stays on your computer. No cloud storage, no training on your data. Privacy by design.

Focus/SIS Export

One-click export to your gradebook. Works with Focus, PowerSchool, Canvas. Sync with OneDrive/SharePoint for automatic collection.

Your Voice, Your Standards

Custom rubrics with your exact criteria. Save assignment configs for reuse. AI learns your teaching style and maintains your voice in every piece of feedback.
